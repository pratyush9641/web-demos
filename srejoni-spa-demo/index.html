V  ·  Good to Know

Questions, answered

Is Srejoni truly ladies-only?
Entirely. Our whole team is female and gentlemen are not seated — so you can be completely at ease, always.
How early should I reserve bridal makeup?
Three to four weeks ahead in wedding season, earlier for popular dates. Your reservation includes a private trial so the look is decided long before the day.
What are your rates?
Honest and reasonable — it's why clients have stayed with us for fifteen years. Message us on WhatsApp and the full rate card arrives in minutes.
Which products do you use?
Trusted professional brands only, chosen for skin and hair safety. Sensitive skin or allergies? Tell us and your ritual is customised.
Do I need a reservation, or may I walk in?
Walk-ins are always welcome. A WhatsApp reservation simply means your chair is waiting — especially on weekends.
