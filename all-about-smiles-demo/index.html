Frequently Asked Questions

Is root canal treatment painful?
Not anymore! With modern anesthesia and rotary equipment, most patients feel no pain at all — many finish in a single visit and are surprised how easy it was.
How much will my treatment cost?
It depends on your specific case, but we always explain the complete cost BEFORE starting any treatment. No hidden charges — that's our promise. Your first consultation includes a clear treatment plan.
Do you treat children?
Yes! We love treating kids and specialise in gentle, fear-free dentistry. Milk teeth problems should never be ignored — they affect the permanent teeth underneath.
What are the clinic timings?

Do I need to pay online to book?
No! Booking is completely free — just send us your details on WhatsApp and we'll confirm your appointment. You only pay at the clinic after your consultation.
